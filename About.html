Nice to meet you, I am Mark!

Who am I?
I am a software development student with over 4 years in mechanical engineering. I have worked in the fields of aerospace, aggregates, and transit doing manufacturing, planning, and designing. After 4 years of experience, I am on my way to getting my Professional Engineer Designation (P.Eng).

Throughout my life (since I was 4 or 5) I was always fascinated with the new features and industry changes that occured in gaming (maybe because I played too many games). Highschool was the first time I ever touched programming. Making my first website was an exciting experience to go from 50 lines of HTML to a functioning webpage. In later years I used Java to build a mancala game, C++ to perform modelling & numerical solutions, and C# to create small games. Now I'm diving head first into software engineering!

My Goals?
To never stop learning! I have an interest in cybersecurity, game development, and programming in general! I would love to create something with code to make people's online lives safer, easier, and more fun!

Here I would like to talk about my hobbies! Even though I have many hobbies - such as cycling, tennis, video games, and astrophotography - I will focus on two hobbies that I have consistently put a lot of love and effort into over the years. One being Dungeons & Dragons, and the other is skiing.

Dungeons & Dragons

I love many aspects of the game, but the one that always keeps me coming back is the expression of creativity. Every campaign and each group has such diverse and unique ideas, and I love bringing new stories and encounters to parties that I play with. Making new friends has also been an amazing experience. I've been able to solidify friendships and meet new people after each new group I play with. One campaign we currently have has been going on for over two years, and isn't slowing down.

Finally, I find that my storytelling and public speaking has improved dramatically. From being nervous to speak to a group of two or three people, to now comfortably being able to manage a party of eight. I have also learned how to understand key plot points in movies and films, and emphasize story elements when storytelling.

Learn more about Dungeons and Dragons here: Wikipedia: Dungeons & Dragons

Skiing

Skiing is one of the sports that I have consistently kept up with my entire life. I started skiing between the ages of 6 and 10, and immediately fell in love. The aspect of being in nature and the thrills of taking on challenging slopes are the reasons why I keep going. I have gone with friends, family, and my partner to many parts of North America to ski. My goal is to one day visit the Swiss Alps and spend a few weeks skiing there. One area that I'd greatly like to improve in is snowboarding. I was able to rent a board a few years ago and found the challenge to be so fun, that I'd love to progress in that field as well.
